Meet DARRELL S. WRIGHT

Estate Planning Law, Probate, Immigration Law.

Darrell is a native Kentuckian and has lived his whole life in the Northern Kentucky area. He graduated from Dixie Heights High School and completed college at Northern Kentucky University. He went on to graduate from Salmon P. Chase College of Law school in 1984.

When asked, he says that he is not sure why he originally decided to attended law school, but he did know that he wanted to help his family and other people. He has always been a hard worker, and put himself through school by working as a server in local restaurants.

His peers described him as an excellent student with a great memory. He raised four children, Gracie, Steven, Matthew and Kelly, and is happily married to his beautiful wife Angie. He currently resides in the Taylor Mill, Kentucky area where he is often seen riding his bicycle.

His entire law career has been with Bartlett & Weigle. He began as a clerk and even volunteered his time for about six months in an effort to prove himself to the team. During that time, he demonstrated his readiness and passion for the job. After passing the bar exam in 1985, he was hired as an attorney. Early in his career, Darrell acquired a great deal of knowledge from those in the firm who were already practicing immigration law. He is now a seasoned attorney with expertise in family immigration law and estate planning.

BAR ADMISSIONS
Admitted to bar, 1985, Kentucky; 1986, Ohio.

EDUCATION
Northern Kentucky University (B.S., 1978); Salmon P. Chase College of Law (J.D. 1984).
