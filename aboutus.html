About Bartlett & Weigle

Bartlett & Weigle Co. L.P.A. is a general practice law firm with a concentration in immigration. Our immigration practice is unique in Cincinnati because we handle every aspect of immigration law including cases involving family, employment, asylum, and removal.

Although we are a firm that concentrates our practice in Immigration, we also have well established practices in other areas of the law such as Business Litigation, Estate Planning, Personal Injury and a substantial Criminal Law practice.

Our Mission

We work every day to deliver creative and effective legal solutions and counsel with the highest quality, efficiency and ethical standards.
