Deportation Defense and Litigation

There is nothing more terrifying for a foreign national than facing detention and deportation from the United States. Individuals facing removal proceedings need aggressive, knowledgeable and experienced attorneys to best represent them and navigate this extremely complex area of law. For over 35 years, the Firm has been successfully representing clients from all over the world in a broad array of matters before the Immigration Judge, Board of Immigration Appeals, and Federal Courts. These matters include, but are not limited to:

  • Obtaining bond and release for individuals, including those who were alleged to be subject to “mandatory detention” by the DHS.
  • Staying deportation and Reopening removal proceedings for individuals previously subject to a final order of deportation and facing imminent removal from the U.S.
  • Challenging and terminating removal proceedings for individuals alleged to have committed a removable criminal offense, fraud, or other immigration violations.
  • Obtaining relief from removal including Cancellation of Removal; Waivers of Inadmissibility; Adjustment of Status; and Asylum and Related Relief.
  • Habeas Corpus and Writ of Mandamus actions in U.S. Federal District Courts.
  • Petitions for Review before the U.S. Courts of Appeal.

Our attorneys are extremely well-regarded and often speak at conferences throughout the country on matters relating to deportation and litigation. Our attorneys have also provided expert testimony in Court cases regarding immigration-related issues. It is critical that those facing removal know their rights and options and consult with competent and experienced immigration attorneys.
